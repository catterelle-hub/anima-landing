What does Anima taste like? +
Anima has a mild, slightly tangy flavour when mixed into plain kefir. Most people find it pleasant and neutral. Some describe it as slightly earthy.
When will I notice a difference? +
Most women report noticing a change in food noise and bloating within 3 to 7 days. Skin and energy changes typically follow in weeks 2 to 4.
Where do I get the kefir? +
Plain, full-fat kefir is available in most supermarkets. Look for live cultures on the label. We recommend avoiding flavoured varieties.
I am lactose intolerant. Can I still use this? +
Many people with lactose sensitivity tolerate kefir well because the fermentation process breaks down most of the lactose. We recommend consulting your doctor first.
Is it safe during pregnancy or breastfeeding? +
We always recommend speaking with your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
I am on medication. Is this safe? +
Anima is a food supplement, not a drug. If you are on medication — particularly for gut, mood, or immune conditions — please speak with your doctor first.
How many boxes do I need? +
We recommend starting with one box (30 days). Most women who continue see compounding improvements through months two and three as the microbiome stabilises.
