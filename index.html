Anima has a mild, slightly tangy flavour from the kefir fermentation process. When mixed into plain kefir, most people describe it as neutral to slightly pleasant. It is not sweet. It is not medicinal. It just blends in.

Most women in our early testing noticed a real difference within 5 to 7 days. Less thinking about food. Lighter digestion. More consistent energy. Not a tweak — a turning point. The full transformation builds over 4 to 8 weeks as your microbiome rebalances.

Plain kefir is available in most supermarkets — Lidl, Aldi, Waitrose, and most health food stores carry it. Look for plain, unsweetened kefir. You only need 150ml per day. We will provide a full guide with your first box.

Fermented kefir has significantly lower lactose than regular dairy, because the bacteria consume most of it during fermentation. Many lactose-sensitive people tolerate kefir well. That said, we recommend starting with a small amount and seeing how your body responds. We will share guidance with your box.

We recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Kefir itself is generally well-tolerated, but individual circumstances vary and your doctor is the right person to advise you.

Anima contains food-grade fibre and fermented compounds. For most people on standard medications, this is not a concern. However, if you are on immunosuppressants or medications that specifically interact with probiotic use, please check with your doctor first.
